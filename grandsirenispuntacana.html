Room Quality & Romantic Upgrades

Spread beautifully across 32 individual low-rise buildings, the resort features 817 accommodations. While standard Double Rooms are clean, bright, and highly functional, they are primarily designed for efficiency and family travel. For couples looking for a true touch of romance, standard layouts can sometimes feel a bit basic.

If you are planning an anniversary, honeymoon, or romantic escape, we highly recommend upgrading your booking to either the Double Romance Room or the Club Premium Mini Suite. These premium tiers feature elegant, updated interiors, cozy king-size beds, and private balconies equipped with outdoor hydromassage jacuzzis overlooking the ocean—providing the perfect private retreat after a day in the sun.

Dining Variety: Exploring the Culinary Options

One of the primary benefits of an all-inclusive stay is the absolute freedom to satisfy any craving. Grand Sirenis provides a robust selection of dining spots across the property:

  • The Main Buffets: Casual options like Macao deliver massive culinary variety with live cooking stations, fresh tropical fruits, pasta bars, and rotating international themes.
  • Themed A La Carte Restaurants: The property hosts 7 specialty a la carte venues, including authentic Italian, vibrant Tex-Mex, and traditional Asian fusion.
  • Lounges & Swim-up Bars: With 13 distinct bars scattered across the resort—including two swim-up pool bars and a dedicated beach bar—a refreshing cocktail is always within arm's reach.

While the overall food profile leans toward practical, crowd-pleasing options rather than fine-dining Michelin stars, the sheer variety ensures that couples will find plenty of delicious options during a week-long stay.

Beach Access and the Uvero Alto Coastline

Grand Sirenis sits directly on a gorgeous stretch of golden sand in the peaceful Uvero Alto region. Unlike crowded downtown resort strips, this beach offers wide-open space, extensive palm trees for natural shade, and complimentary sun loungers.

The ocean water here is beautifully clear and shallow near the shore, making it wonderful for a romantic morning stroll or relaxing in the surf. For active couples, your all-inclusive wristband gives you free access to non-motorized water sports, letting you grab a double kayak, try windsurfing, or head out with snorkel gear right from the sand.

💦 The Water Park Factor: The resort includes access to the massive *Sirenis Aquagames* water park. While it is a major draw for families during the day, couples can easily choose to skip the slides and relax at the main lagoon-style pools or find quiet spots on the expansive beach.

Nightly Entertainment & Activities

When the sun sets, the resort comes alive with a production level that sets it apart from typical Caribbean properties. Rather than basic or amateurish sketches, the on-site theater features professional dancers, impressive set designs, and high-energy musical reviews that offer genuine entertainment. Afterwards, couples can head to the Plaza bar or hit the dance floor at the resort's nightclub to keep the evening going.
