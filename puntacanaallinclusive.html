Deconstructing the Package: What's Typically Included?

While fine print can change from resort to resort, standard all-inclusive models in Punta Cana are built to cover the essential components of your day-to-day stay. Here is exactly what you get for your money:

  • Unlimited Meals & Snacks: This covers full access to the primary international breakfast, lunch, and dinner buffets, along with casual poolside grills and late-night snack venues.
  • Beverages & Spirits: Standard packages include unlimited non-alcoholic juices, sodas, and water, along with local beers, house wines, and classic tropical cocktails.
  • Daily Activities & Nightly Entertainment: This spans on-site beach volleyball tournaments, dance lessons, and fitness classes during the afternoon, plus live theater productions, music, and beach parties once the sun goes down.
  • Non-Motorized Water Sports: Enjoy complimentary use of windsurfers, kayaks, stand-up paddleboards, and snorkeling equipment right from the resort's beachfront.
⚠️ The Out-of-Pocket Checklist: Motorized water sports (like jet skiing or parasailing), high-end spa treatments, top-shelf imported liquors, and romantic candlelit dinners on the beach usually incur an additional surcharge. Always audit the property amenities list before confirming.
