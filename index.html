Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful answers for travelers searching for Punta Cana all inclusive, all exclusive Punta Cana, and Dominican Republic resort deals.

What is included in Punta Cana all inclusive vacations?

Most include lodging, meals, drinks, pools, beach access, resort amenities, and entertainment. Some resorts may charge extra for premium restaurants, spa services, excursions, motorized water sports, or airport transfers.

Is Punta Cana good for all inclusive resorts?

Yes. Punta Cana is one of the top Caribbean destinations for all inclusive resorts, with many options for couples, families, groups, honeymoons, weddings, and luxury vacations.

Should I book hotel-only or a vacation package?

Check both. Hotel-only deals can be best when you already have flights, while vacation packages may save money when bundling flights and resorts together.

Are there family-friendly all inclusive resorts in Punta Cana?

Yes. Many resorts offer kids clubs, family pools, water parks, beach activities, casual restaurants, and larger room options.

Are there adults-only all inclusive resorts in Punta Cana?

Yes. Punta Cana has many adults-only options for couples, honeymoons, romantic getaways, quiet beach trips, and luxury escapes.
